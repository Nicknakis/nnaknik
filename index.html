About Me

I hold a PhD from the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) in the Section of Cognitive Systems. Following my doctoral studies, I was a postdoctoral researcher at École Polytechnique in the Polytechnic Institute of Paris, where I worked in the Data Science and Mining Lab. Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher at Yale Institute for Network Science in the Human Nature Lab. My research focuses on machine learning for graphs, particularly graph representation learning and statistical learning. I apply these methods to areas such as social network analysis, network polarization, and developing self-explainable models to enhance interpretability and transparency in AI systems..
